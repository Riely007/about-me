Riely Chen

I'm a generalist and ecosystem developer. I work at Geode Labs leading ecosystem development and writing about global Ethereum adoption at Local Ethereum. Previously, I worked at Ethereum Foundation on ecosystem development.

I've been connecting the western and eastern worlds for a decade building bridges between communities, institutions, and the people who make open technology move.

My life's work is to introduce talented people to the latest technology, and more recently, Ethereum.

Things I believe
  • Health is the foundation. A clear mind and a strong body compound faster than any investment.
  • Love: take care of the people around you, family and friends matter.
  • Optimize for freedom: physical, financial, intellectual. Design your life before someone else does it for you.
  • Knowledge should be accessible. YouTube and Z-Library are amazing.
  • Seeking deeper truths together with value-aligned people is one of the most wonderful things in the world.
  • Slow FIRE: do work that matters, earn enough, and retire early.
  • Nature is a blessing. European summers are the best.
